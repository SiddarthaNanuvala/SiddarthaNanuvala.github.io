01 — About Me

Building systems that actually hold up

Not just demo well — production ready, monitored, and secure from day one.

I'm finishing my Master's at ESILV Paris (École Supérieure d'Ingénieurs Léonard de Vinci), somewhere between Cloud, DevOps, and AI. Most of my time goes into data pipelines, deployment, and the security layer underneath the actual model.

The project I'm proudest of is a RAG system I built with a team for a BNP Paribas Cardif use case. My part was the retrieval layer and the FastAPI service: chunking documents, indexing them in FAISS, generating answers through Gemini, and getting the whole thing running with CI/CD that didn't require babysitting.

On my own, I built a real-time anomaly detection system that processes over 1,000 events per second in under a millisecond, trained on 2.8M network flow samples with PyTorch. Getting it from a training script to something monitored and reliable on Kubernetes taught me more than the model itself did.

I'm also certified in ISO/IEC 27001 & 27005, so security tends to show up early in what I build — not as a patch afterward.
